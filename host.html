The Dhandho Investor

In Mohnish's book, the Dhandho Investor, he talks about his investment philosophy at length. Among the most important points:

  1. Look for "no brainers"- or 0 risk with unlimited reward (Dhandho)
  2. Focus on "arbitrage" opportunities, especially in entrepreneurship
  3. Bet heavily when odds are in your favor
  4. Heads I win tails I don't lose much mentality
  5. Look towards opportunities under distress and uncertainty
As an immigrant to America, Mohnish uses many immigrants as examples on how to do investing and entrepreneurship in a low-risk high-reward manner.
